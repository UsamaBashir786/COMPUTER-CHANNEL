Bilkul! Hum properly formatted PDF quotation dete hain jis mein item list, specs, prices, aur stamp/signature hoti hai. Yeh procurement departments aur school committees ke liye directly acceptable hoti hai.
5 units se bulk pricing start hoti hai. 10+ units par special discount milta hai aur 30+ units par dedicated delivery + setup support bhi available hai. WhatsApp par quantity batayein aur hum custom quote karenge.
Har machine par manufacturer warranty hoti hai (HP/Dell/Lenovo ki official warranty). Claim ke liye seedha humari shop par aa jain — hum process guide karte hain. Local support ka faida yahi hai.
Bulk corporate orders ke liye 50% advance aur 50% delivery pe arrangement ho sakti hai. Individual purchases ke liye shop par aake baat karein — hum flexible hain.
